NYC Administrative Code
=================================

This document contains the codified local laws of New York City. The `Constitution of New York <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitution_of_New_York>`_ enumerates the powers of `local governments <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_New_York>`_, such as the power to elect a legislative body and adopt local laws. A local law has a status equivalent with a law enacted by the Legislature (subject to certain exceptions and restrictions).
