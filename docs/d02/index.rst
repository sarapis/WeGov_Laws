Rules of NYC
=================================

This document contains the compiled rules and regulations (`delegated legislation <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delegated_legislation>`_) of `New York City <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City>`_ government agencies. `[1] <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City_Rules#cite_note-Gibson-1>`_ `[2] <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City_Rules#cite_note-rules.cityofnewyork.us-2>`_ It contains approximately 6000 rules and regulations in 71 titles, each covering a different City agency. `[1] <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City_Rules#cite_note-Gibson-1>`_ `[2] <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City_Rules#cite_note-rules.cityofnewyork.us-2>`_ The City Record is the official journal of New York City. `[3] <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City_Rules#cite_note-3>`_ `[4] <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City_Rules#cite_note-4>`_
